HE HOALOHALOHA NO KE KULA 
SABATI O HELANI, NO IOSEPA
KAHOOLANA NAHALEPAINIU.

Mr. ame Mrs. J. K. Nahale.,
Paumako wale:
E hooholoia, ma o ko makou komite
`la:- oiai, o makou o na haumana o 
ke "Kula Sabati o Helani," e aoho ana
mai "Kahaluu" i ka "Waipukaiki o
Helani," a i "Keahou." I ka "Owe ne-
he a ko kai i ka iliili."
E hooholoia, oiaim me na naau i hoo-
pihaia me ka luuluu, kaumaha, ehaeha,
a makuamakuahua i ka lohe ana mai,
no ka hala a nalowale loa ana aku o 
Joseph Kahoolana Nahalopalulu, mai
ke alo aku o olua, o na makua aloha,
ma ka la 33 o Mei, M. H. 1899, ma ke
Kula o Kamehameha, Iwaena wale iho
ko o kona mau kumu ame kona mau 
hea kula, me ke koe iho hoi o olaa o 
na makua aloha me ka ike ole hoi i 
kona manawa hope loa.
Auwe! ka mea ehaeha
E hooholoia, oiai, o Joseph Kahoo-
lana Nahalepainiu i hala iho`la i ka
polikua`kane, he oalo wale iho na oia,
i kau nui ia aku na manaolana ana he
nui maluna ona, no na la e hiki mai
ana, aka, ua wehe ka pili hookao ia lo-
ko, ola keiki kupa, ola keiki kamaai-
na, a ola keiki papa ponoi loa au e
"Kona Kaiopua i ka lai." "Opa hina-
no ua malie." "Hiolo na waimaka o ke 
kehau."
E hooholoia, oiai, o makou o kona 
mau hoa haumana Kula Sabati o He-
lani, ua hookui puia mai makou, me
kekahi puupuu ikaika loa, a aole a ike
hou ia aku kona aloalo hou ana mai i
kou mau maka`lae e Kona, aole hoi i
kou mau kipuka kai kaheka e ka heiau
a Keeku, a aole hoi i kou kula anuu-
nuu e Kahaluu, kahi ana e aloalo ai
me kona makua. Ua hala, ua nalo, a
ua pau loa kona lohe hou ana i kou
Bele e Helani, a ua pau pu hoi me kou
holue ana iho i ka malumalu o kou
mau paia eehia maluna ona.
E hooholoia, ke komo pu a ke hui
pu a ke kumakena pu aku nei ke "Ku-
la Sabati holookoa o Helani," me na
makua i hooneleia, me ke kaikuaana i
nele iho`la, me na pokii i noho-u iho
la, ame na kupuna e kanikau aku nei,
a maluna ae o na mea apau, e hooma-
naoia.
Love of God with you all, Amen.
HENRY L. KAWEWEHI,
